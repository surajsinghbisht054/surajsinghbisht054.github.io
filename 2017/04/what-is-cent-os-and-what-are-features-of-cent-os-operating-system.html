Q 1. What Is Cent OS?.


Ans. CentOS is a Linux Distribution Based On Linux Operating System Developed By The CentOS Project Team and affiliated with Red Hat. The First CentOS was released In May 2004. CentOS operating system is Completely free to use and Cent OS Provides us a free computing platform that is supported by CentOS Community and Red Hat Enterprise Linux. CentOS Linux is no-cost and free to redistribute. Each CentOS version is maintained for up to 10 years. In January 2014, Cent OS Announced the official joining with Red Hat while staying independent from Red Hat Enterprise Linux.

first,
